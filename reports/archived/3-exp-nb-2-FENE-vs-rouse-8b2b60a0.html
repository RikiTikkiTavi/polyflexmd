Rouse time estimated analytically¶

Svaneborg (15)

Rouse relaxation time: $$ \tau_R = \frac{1}{3 \pi^2} \frac{\zeta N_b^2 l_b^2}{k_B T}$$ Relaxation time of single bead: $$ \tau_0 = \frac{3 \pi^2 \tau_R}{N_b^2} $$
