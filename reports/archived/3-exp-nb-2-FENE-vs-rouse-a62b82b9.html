Rouse time estimated analytically¶

Svaneborg (15)

Rouse relaxation time: $$ \tau_R = \frac{1}{3 \pi^2} \frac{\zeta N_b^2 l_b^2}{k_B T}$$ Relaxation time of single bead: $$ \tau_0 = \frac{\tau_R}{N_b^2} $$

In [26]:
zeta = 1  # LJ
k_B = 1
T = 1
tau_rouse = N_bonds_rouse ** 2 * l_b ** 2 * zeta / (3 * np.pi ** 2 * k_B * T)
